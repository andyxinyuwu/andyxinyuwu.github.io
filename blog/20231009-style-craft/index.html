StyleCraft

09 Oct, 2023

“Element of Style” is a classic guide for writing non-fiction. The author emphasizes clear and concise writing to ensure readers can easily understand the writer’s ideas.

While the book is helpful, it takes years of intentional practice to effectively implement the author’s guidance. With the advent of Gen AI, however, we now have the potential to input the author’s instructions into a simple prompt and use it to revise articles. Inspired by this idea, I have created a tool called StyleCraft. You can try it out here and view the source code here. This tool is intended to benefit professionals who write emails, product documents, or research papers for work, as well as individuals who write personal blogs or non-fiction books.

This blog is revised using this tool.
